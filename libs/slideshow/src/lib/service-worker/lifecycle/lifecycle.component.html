Service Worker Thread

Know This

  • Workers are "scoped" to the path they were lodaed from
  • Service worker will not be activated until all clients are closed
  • You can `.skipWaiting()` and claim clients immediately
  • There is a 24 hour fail-safe

Updates Happen When:

  • You visit a page in the scope
  • `push` or `sync` events happen
  • The URL changes
